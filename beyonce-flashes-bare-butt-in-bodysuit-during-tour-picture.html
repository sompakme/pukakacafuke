Publish date: 2024-07-21

Business in the front, party in the back! Beyonce truly brought it during the opening night of her On the Run tour with husband Jay Z on Wednesday, June 25, performing hit after hit and stunning the crowd in a particularly eye-catching ensemble.

Queen Bey donned a number of outfit changes throughout the night, but one in particular stood out—a chaps-like black bodysuit with butt cutouts designed by Givenchy.

Although we're not too sure what we think about the look, Jay Z's wife's assets couldn't have looked better.

When not baring her booty, the songstress wore a black sequined Atelier Versace fishnet bodysuit and a colorful long-sleeve Atelier Versace-print leotard with a black-and-gold logo knit beanie.
