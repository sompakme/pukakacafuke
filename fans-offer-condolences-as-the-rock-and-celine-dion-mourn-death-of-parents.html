Fans Offer Condolences As The Rock And Celine Dion Mourn Death Of Parents

Publish date: 2024-07-18

The past few days have proven to be incredibly painful and difficult ones for fan-favorites Dwayne (The Rock) Johnson and Celine Dion.

Johnson is currently mourning the loss of his father as Celine grieves the loss of her mother, and fans rush to send messages of condolences and support as they discover this tragic news.

In a touching display of raw emotion, fans watched as The Rock crumbled at the news of his father’s passing.

Entertainment Tonight posted his loving tribute to his father and quotes Johnson as he thanks his dad by saying, “I’m grateful that he gave me life and life's invaluable lessons." Wayde Douglas Bowles, WWE Hall of Famer and known in the wrestling world as Rocky Johnson, passed away at the age of 75.

It was hard to hold back tears as Dwayne poured his love out on social media, posting; "Dad, I wish I had one more shot to tell you, I love you, before you crossed over to the other side but you were ripped away from me so fast without warning. Gone in an instant and no coming back."

While fans reeled at the news of the passing of Dwayne’s father, they had little time to brace themselves before the media began to report that Celine Dion’s mother had also passed away.

In a tragically sad coincidence, Therese Dion passed away on the 4th anniversary of the passing of Celine’s brother, Daniel.

Just when you thought the chills up your spine couldn’t rattle any more, TMZ reports the date of her passing was just 2 days after the 4th anniversary of the death of Rene Angelil, Celine Dion’s late husband. The 92-year-old, affectionately known in Canada as “Mama Dion” was 92.

One common thread between the two tragic losses is the huge outpouring of support shown by fans across the world. Messages of condolences, affection, adoration, and support are plentiful and are sure to provide much-needed comfort to Dwayne Johnson and Celine Dion during this difficult time.
