Publish date: 2024-08-12

David Walton's a dad!

The Bent actor's wife, Majandra Delfino, gave birth to the couple's first child last week, daughter Cecilia Delphine Walton, Walton's rep confirms to Us Weekly.

Baby Cecilia was born June 14, and weighed in at 9 lbs, 3 oz.

In January, Walton's rep told Us the star and his wife of over a year were thrilled to learn that Delfino was expecting.
