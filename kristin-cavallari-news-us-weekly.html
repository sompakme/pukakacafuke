Kristin Cavallari News - Us Weekly

Publish date: 2024-09-05

Name:Kristin Cavallari

Hometown:Laguna Beach, California

Relationship:Single

Birthday:January 5, 1987

About

Born in Denver, Kristin Cavallari moved to Laguna Beach to live with her father after her freshman year of high school. As a junior, she began starring on the MTV reality series Laguna Beach and quickly won the hearts of viewers.

Cavallari was involved in an on-and-off relationship with castmate Stephen Colletti, causing a rivalry between her and costar Lauren Conrad. She went on to star on the spinoff series The Hills from 2006 to 2009 and later launched her own reality series Very Cavallari, which aired for three seasons from 2018 to 2020. In 2022, Cavallari started a Laguna Beach rewatch podcast with Colletti titled “Back to the Beach With Kristin and Stephen.”

Along with Colletti, Cavallari has dated professional quarterback Matt Leinart, fellow reality star Brody Jenner, singer Nick Lachey and MTV’s Nick Zano. She sparked a romance with former Chicago Bears quarterback Jay Cutler in 2010, whom she married in 2013.

Cavallari and Cutler welcomed sons Camden and Jaxon in 2012 and 2014, respectively, and daughter Saylor in 2015. The twosome called it quits in April 2020 after seven years of marriage. Following her divorce, Cavallari has been romantically linked to comedian Jeff Dye and country singer Chase Rice.
