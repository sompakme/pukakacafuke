Torrey DeVitto, Rick Glassman Split After More Than a Year

Publish date: 2024-08-20

Pretty Little Liars’ Torrey DeVitto and Undateable actor Rick Glassman have called it quits on their relationship, DeVitto’s rep exclusively confirms to Us Weekly.

According to an insider, DeVitto, 31, and Glassman broke up due to distance. “Her being in Chicago and him in L.A. put a strain on their relationship, so they amicably split,” the source tells Us. “They were both really into each other and it was hard for both of them.”

Us exclusively revealed the two were dating in January 2015. They first stepped out together at the HBO Luxury Lounge at the Four Seasons in Beverly Hills, where they were spotted being openly affectionate and kissing.
