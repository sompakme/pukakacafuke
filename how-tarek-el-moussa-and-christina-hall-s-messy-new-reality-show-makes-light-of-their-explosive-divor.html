HGTV favorites Tarek El Moussa and Christina Hall are set to reunite for a new show, The Flip Off, set to air in 2025. The couple found fame on Flip or Flop and started a flipping revolution. However, the couple announced their separation in 2016 and were officially divorced in 2018.

The new show will see Tarek El Moussa and his wife Heather Rae El Moussa compete against Christina Hall and her realtor husband Joshua Hall. This will be the first time the TV stars have appeared on a flipping show together since 2022. In recent years, they have appeared on separate shows, although they still manage to create tension and arguments.

All four of the TV personalities taking part teased the project with an Instagram post poking fun at how similar Christina Hill and Heather Rae El Moussa look.

In the following, we take a look at Chritina and Tarek's new show with their current partners. We also take a look back at what happened to their marriage and how the HGTV stars reconciled.

What Tarek El Moussa And Christina Hall's New Reality Show, The Flip Off Is About

Tarek El Moussa and Christina Hall are set to reunite on TV for their new show, The Flip Off. The show, which is set to air in 2025, will see Tarek and Christina alongside their new spouses, Selling Sunset's Heather Rae El Moussa and Joshua Hall.

The couples will compete to find, buy, and flip a house for the most money. "Tarek El Moussa and Christina Hall built a home-flipping empire and top HGTV series with Flip or Flop. But it all came crashing down after their much-publicized divorce," reads the official synopsis.

"Now, the exes are back — along with their new spouses — for a throw down competition to see who can find, buy, renovate, and flip a house for the biggest financial gain, and the chance at bragging rights."

The Flip Off will premiere in early 2025 with a super-sized premiere, and HGTV promises that the exes “are sure to bring the flipping drama.”

Tarek El Moussa And Christina Hall's Divorce Changed Everything

Close

Tarek El Moussa and Christina Hall met in 2005 while working as real estate agents in California. The pair tied the knot in 2009 and welcomed their first child in September in 2010.

The pair found fame when Flip or Flop made its debut on HGTV in 2013. In the series, Tarek discovers homes to flip, and Christina focuses on decorating the space. The series jumpstarted the trend of married couples buying and flipping houses on TV. If the couples were having issues, it did not come across on their television show.

Fans of the El Moussas were shocked when they announced their separation in 2016, after seven years of marriage. They continued to make Flip or Flop together, as well as producing Chi-Town Flip which followed Bryan and Maira Segal as they flipped Chicago properties.

Later it was revealed that Christina feared for Tarek's life when his mental health plummeted. After an explosive argument between the pair, Tarek alleges he left the couple's home for a hike, taking his .39 caliber pistol with him. Fearing that he would harm himself, Christina called 9-1-1. This incident led to the demise of their family and caused Tarek to enter a halfway house for two months.

Tarek has explained that in the weeks leading up to his divorce were "physical and emotional hell," describing bouts with alcohol and struggling with an addiction to testosterone. He holds no negative thoughts about Christina, understanding why she called emergency services despite it causing him to be handcuffed and the story hitting the headlines.

"I hold nothing against Christina. I understand why she did what she did. We had a lot of hard years through my sicknesses and my mental health struggles from the testosterone… I've never said any of this, ever."

The pair continued to try to work together, even after their dramatic split. While on set for Flip or Flop in the summer of 2021, Tarek El Moussa verbally assaulted Christina Hall. He called her a lesser version of his wife, a former Playboy model who recently appeared in Netflix's Selling Sunset and referred to Hall as a "washed-up loser."

In March 2022, El Moussa and Hall confirmed that Flip or Flop was ending."I will be forever grateful to have had a series for a decade," Christina Hall explained to PEOPLE. "It's a huge accomplishment and everyone who worked on the show should be very proud."

El Moussa added that "I couldn't be more grateful for the last ten years with Flip Or Flop. The support from our fans, the network and the wild ride that it's been has been incredible."

"Even though the Flip Or Flop chapter is coming to a close, another exciting one is soon starting."

Tarek El Moussa and Christina Hall hosted 148 episodes of Flip or Flop together on HGTV from 2013-2022.

How Tarek El Moussa And Christina Hall Reconciled

Tarek El Moussa and Christina Hall moved on from one another, finding love and building new families. This perhaps allowed them to reconcile for TV. The last time the duo appeared on a TV show together was in Flip or Flop: The Final Flip, which aired in November 2022.

"I never thought I’d be doing this," Christina says in a voiceover for the trailer to The Flip Off.

"This is one of the defining moments of my life," Tarek adds in the 30 second trailer "Did you try to dismiss me? You thought I was history. But now you gotta deal with me. I’m back with a vengeance," which plays as Tarek and Heather step out of gleaming cars in all-black outfits.
