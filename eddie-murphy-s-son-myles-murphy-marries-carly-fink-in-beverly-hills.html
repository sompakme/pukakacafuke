Eddie Murphy's Son, Myles Murphy, Marries Carly Fink In Beverly Hills

Publish date: 2024-08-12

Eddie Murphy‘s son, Myles Murphy, married his fiancée Carly Fink on Saturday (Sept. 9). The private wedding occurred in Beverly Hills, Calif., and featured attendees such as the comedy legend and his ex-wife Nicole Murphy. Myles and Carly’s 4-year-old daughter, Evie, also attended the ceremony. PEOPLE reported that Myles wore a Dion Julian Lattimore tuxedo while his bride donned an Enzoani bridal gown. 

Nicole included an Instagram carousel showing off the ceremony’s sights and sounds. “Congratulations to my son Myles & now his beautiful wife Carly. The wedding was stunning. Proud of you two,” Niki lovingly typed. “One big happy family.” Images showed off the Murphy family matriarchy dressed in soft pink dresses. Photos also showed a smiling Eddie and Nicole’s new mysterious beau. 

Ahead of the ceremony, Carly showed her then-future husband some birthday love. In November 2022, Carly hit IG with a tribute to Myles on his 30th birthday. “30. Myles, the love Evie & I have for you is infinite! I am SO proud of you always,” she typed. “God will continue to bless you & our whole family with health, happiness, success & blessings. I love you my FIANCÉ & I can’t wait to marry you.❤️ HAPPY BIRTHDAY BABY!”

A couple of months before that, in July 2022, Myles proposed to Fink. The clip was caught on camera and showed the pair on a boat before the writer got on one knee. “Forever my love,” she captioned the video. 

Then, in July 2023, the bride-to-be showed more love to her man on Father’s Day. Fink uploaded pictures featuring their daughter, Evie, having fun with her father, Myles. “Happy Father’s Day to our number 1 guy. I will continue to thank God for blessing us with you. We ADORE you babe, THANK YOU for being the best of the best. Evie and I love & appreciate you SO much!”
