Who Was Yvette Mimieuxs Ex-Husband Stanley Donen?

Publish date: 2024-08-03

Stanley Donen was an American film director and choreographer whose most celebrated works include; On the Town and Singin’ in the Rain. He was married to Actress, Yvette Mimieux from 1972 to 1985.

Stanley Donen death

Is Stanley Donen alive? When did Stanley Donen die? Stanley Donen died on February 21, 2019.

Stanley Donen cause of death

What was Stanley Donen’s cause of death? Stanley Donen died from heart failure.

Stanley Donen age

How old was Stanley Donen when he died? Stanley Donen died at the age of 94.

Stanley Donen film produced

What are some of the films produced by Stanley Donen? Stanley Donen produced quite a lot of films. Some of them are;

  • Charade (1963)
  • Two for the Road (1967)
  • Arabesque (1966)
  • Saturn 3 (1980)

Stanley Donen children

Did Stanley Donen have any children? Who are Stanley Donen’s children? Stanley Donen had three children before he died. However, none of them was mothered by Yvette Mimieux. The children are;

  • Peter Donen.
  • Joshua Donen.
  • Mark Donen.
