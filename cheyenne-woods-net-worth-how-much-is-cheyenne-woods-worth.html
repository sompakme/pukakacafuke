Cheyenne Woods’ Education and Early Career

During her formative years, Cheyenne Woods demonstrated her talent and dedication to golf, setting the stage for a successful career. Starting with her education, she attended Xavier College Preparatory, where she not only excelled academically but also became an outstanding member of the school’s golf team. Her exceptional skills on the golf course led her to victory in consecutive Arizona 5A State Championships in 2006 and 2007.

Building on her achievements, Cheyenne went on to pursue higher education at Wake Forest University. While studying at the esteemed university, she continued to showcase her golfing prowess, earning numerous wins in amateur tournaments. Her commitment and skill on the fairways earned her a place in the national spotlight, setting the stage for her transition into professional golf.

After graduating from college, Cheyenne Woods made the pivotal decision to turn professional in 2012, marking the beginning of her promising career as a professional golfer.
