Tammy Pescatelli, born on May 15, 1969, is an American stand-up comic known for being the champion of Comedy Central’s Stand Up Showdown. She is the co-creator of a tv variety show called A Standup Mum. She was chosen four years in a row to be included in the best jokes of the past year in the New York Post.

In 2004, during the second and third seasons of Last Comic Standing, she was a finalist.
