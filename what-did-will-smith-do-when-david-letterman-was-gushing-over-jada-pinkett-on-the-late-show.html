"Violence in all of its forms is poisonous and destructive. My behavior at last night’s Academy Awards was unacceptable and inexcusable. Jokes at my expense are a part of the job, but a joke about Jada’s medical condition was too much for me to bear and I reacted emotionally."

Fans are eager to see what takes place during the interview, but for the time being, let's look back at a memorable moment from the 'Late Show', which features Will Smith's hilarious reaction to Dave gushing over pics of his wife.

Will Smith Poked Fun At David Letterman For Checking Out Jada's Magazine Photos

During the interview, David Letterman pulls out the 'Essence' Magazine, which has Jada and Will on the cover.

"Oh here we go, my goodness," David Letterman says looking at the cover. "Do you mind if we take a look at this?"

Smith responds, "Hey, hey, I don't like what's in your voice, what are you talking about man."

"You looking at my woman with all the stuff on," Will further says as the audience laughs.

"I can look at her, she's in a magazine," Letterman says, "just lower your tone," Smith hilariously responds.

Will Smith Left To India After The 'Oscar' Slap

Will Smith has kept very silent and as it turns out, he took a spiritual trip to India, in an effort to better his mental health. Will was seen overseas, and he was all smiles with the fans. Hopefully, such a trip can aid in giving the actor some mental clarity, in the midst of the bumpiest time of his career.

Jada would also briefly touch on the matter, stating that the family will come forward and discuss the matter in public when the time is right.

Some of the discoveries around our healing will be shared at the table when the time calls," read the statement. "Until then... the table will continue offering itself to powerful, inspiring and healing testimonies like that of our incredibly impressive first guest. Thanks for joining us, Jada."
