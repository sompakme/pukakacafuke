Kenny Omega Confronts AEW Ref Rick Knox, NJPW Collection App Announced

Publish date: 2024-09-22

Kenny Omega Confronts AEW Ref Rick Knox

The newest episode of Being the Elite helped fix one of All Elite Wrestling Dynamite’s biggest gaffes in their TNT debut.

In the episode, Kenny Omega confronts referee Rick Knox about him not ending The Elite’s tag match against Chris Jericho and the former LAX members after Jon Moxley attacked him. Knox says, “Nobody wants a main event to end in a DQ.” Omega then reiterated that wins and losses count, so the referees need to do their job.

NJPW Collection App Announced

New Japan Pro-Wrestling is releasing a new app called NJPW Collection. In it, players can unlock cards “depicting wrestlers and moments from New Japan’s past and present.” It will also feature plenty of news on the promotion and clips from their shows.
