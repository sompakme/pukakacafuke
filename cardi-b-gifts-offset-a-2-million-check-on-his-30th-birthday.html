Cardi B Gifts Offset A $2 Million Check On His 30th Birthday

Publish date: 2024-07-13

Rapper Offset got one heck of a surprise at his 30th birthday bash this week after his wife Cardi B presented him with a giant check. The rapper looked stunned as she announced that she was giving him $2 Million to spend on his upcoming business ventures.

"What do you like? Oh, that's right! Some schmoney gang!" Cardi B announced as she presented her husband with a giant check

Offset, who is part of the hip hop trio Migos, actually turned 30 last week but was treated to a lavish celebration to commemorate the milestone birthday. Offset wasn’t the only attendee to walk away with cash that night as Cardi B announced that they would be throwing $100,000 to party attendees.

The event was dubbed the ‘Sneaker Ball’ and featured a backdrop of sneakers and roses for attendees to take photos in front of as well as a variety of different sneaker-themed art installations.

Among the attendees were Quavo, Kanye West, French Montana, The Game, and NLE Choppa.

Offset and Cardi B arrived at the ball in style, with Cardi wearing a re-worked sneaker corset, black pants, and tons of bling on her neck including a stunning Playboy bunny necklace in celebration of her role as Playboy’s new creative director. Offset kept it classic with a baby blue Louis Vuitton jacket, light wash jeans, and sneakers.

“Babe, this is my birthday gift to you. I know you have a lot of business ventures coming in 2022 so bring out the birthday gift m****f****!" Cardi B said as a giant check was brought out. "That's $2 million, N****. Here you go!"

While it’s unclear what business ventures Offset has in the works, previously he has been involved in a partnership with AXSD media to develop a new streaming platform and production company, as well as a performance series featuring emerging artists.

In a video of the event posted by Cardi B, Offset was seen showing off his giant check. Cardi captioned the moment: "Happy birthday!!! He literally got it all."

For what it's worth, Offset isn't exactly struggling with money. He reportedly has a net worth of$ 26 million. A few months ago for Cardi B’s birthday, the rapper purchased her a Dominican mansion.
