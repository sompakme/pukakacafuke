Cardi B (born Belcalis Almanzar) has come a long way from her humble beginnings in the Bronx. Despite growing up in poverty, she has managed to amass a mega net worth of $80 million. She has been seen at numerous high-profile events wearing designer clothing and has even collaborated with brands like Reebok to release her own collections.

Additionally, Cardi B has used her platform to speak out on important social issues, such as politics and racial inequality. Her outspokenness and authenticity have earned her a loyal fan base and cemented her as a cultural icon.

How Did Cardi B Start Earning Money?

Social media allowed Cardi B to get discovered and land a role on Love & Hip Hop

Despite facing a number of setbacks early on in life, Cardi B managed to turn things around by becoming a successful musician and businesswoman, amassing an $80 million net worth. While many may have judged her for her past as a stripper, she has been open about how it served as an important stepping stone towards achieving her goals. By being honest about her struggles and working hard to overcome them, Cardi has become an inspiring figure to many and a testament to the power of perseverance and self-belief.

Social media has revolutionized the entertainment industry by providing a platform for aspiring artists to showcase their talents. One such artist who owes much of her success to social media is Cardi B. With her unique personality and witty Cardi-isms, she quickly gained a massive following on social media, catching the attention of producers and ultimately landing a spot on VH1's Love & Hip Hop.

Cardi B's rise to fame began when she was discovered and joined the cast of Love & Hip Hop: New York in 2015. Her magnetic personality and unapologetic attitude quickly made her a fan-favorite on the show. As her popularity grew, so did her paycheck. By 2016, it is likely that she was earning the higher end of the expected salary range for the show.

According to StyleCaster,Cardi B made between $200,000 to $300,000 per season in 2020. It's safe to say that Cardi B proved her worth and then some during her time on Love & Hip Hop: New York.

How Much Did Cardi B Earn From Her First Album?

Cardi B's income for Invasion of Privacy is unknown

Cardi B's debut album, Invasion of Privacy, was a massive success both commercially and critically. The album was released on April 6, 2018, and debuted at No. 1 on the Billboard 200 chart. It featured popular songs like "Bodak Yellow" and "I Like It", which made history as the first two songs by a female rapper to reach No. 1 on the Billboard Hot 100.

The record also became the first album by a female artist to chart 13 songs simultaneously on the Billboard Hot 100. Then, a year after its release, Invasion of Privacy became the first album from a solo female rapper to win the Grammy Award for Best Rap Album, solidifying Cardi B as a trailblazer and a force to be reckoned with in hip-hop. According to HipHopDx, Cardi has made “millions” (plural) from the album, making her Love & Hip Hop paycheck look like nothing.

How Many Endorsements Does Cardi B Have?

Cardi B has made millions off her endorsement deals

Cardi B made $1 million in one day for her fashion line with FashionNova. FashionNova is a popular clothing brand known for its trendy and affordable pieces. In an interview with People Magazine, the rapper-turned-fashion designer expressed her admiration for the company's work ethic and organizational skills. She also commended FashionNova for catering to her needs and fulfilling all her requests. One of Cardi's main concerns was to ensure that the clothing line felt high-end and not too revealing or provocative while reflecting her personal style and appealing to a diverse audience. Needless to say, it was a huge success for the rapper.

Cardi B has also partnered with several other brands for endorsement deals. The endorsements include:

Additionally, Cardi B has released her own line of vodka-infused whip shots in three flavors: vanilla, caramel, and mocha. And, of course, fans cannot forget her Nickelodeon debut.

In April 2022, Cardi B, also known as 'Sharki B', made her debut on the popular Nickelodeon show Baby Shark's Hit Show, alongside Offset (or 'Offshark') and Kulture (aka 'Kulture Shark').

During the episode, she performed a new song called "The Seaweed Sway". All in all, Cardi is making millions from endorsements as well.
