Meek Mill Slams 50 Cent For Attacking Diddy's Son, King Combs

Publish date: 2024-08-10

Meek Mill isn’t letting 50 Cent get away with dissing King Combs. On Monday (May 13), Meek jumped in between Fif and Diddy‘s son as the G-Unit boss reacted to his diss song, “Pick a Side.” King’s song called out Fif and “many men” who have waited decades to “tear” down his father. He then called out federal agents for raiding Sean Combs’ homes, which resulted in King and his brother, Justin, being arrested. 

“When all they had was 50 Cent, who put the city on the map?/ Stop lying, pops been hated on by many men and n***a, that’s fine/ They gon’ try to stop these Eminems and they gon’ die tryin,” King Combs rapped. 

“Knock them walls down like when them feddy boys raided both of our cribs/ Too bad they ain’t know we bought the one next door ’cause that’s the one they missed,” he later added.

Naturally, 50 began trolling King but Meek isn’t feeling his energy toward a “lil boy.” “Because your federal the street ni**as in Queens know that’s why you tryna pick on a lil boy,” Meek Mill wrote to 50 Cent on X (formerly known as Twitter). “Your own son hate your guts lol I’m so grateful to be on a island with my son and his friends on his bday… don’t follow these guys they miserable lives be terrible.”

“Yall ni**as 50 online beefing with kids… of course yall ni**as left all yall friends and family behind! Yall holding the culture back fr old hating manipulation ni**as lol.”

50 Cent shot back and and clowned on Meek Mill for his last project suffering from low album sales. “You sold 6k copies your last project, you should not be on Vacation,” he typed. “Still chasing the dream or embracing the nightmare. Standing by your man, that I respect!”

Meek doubled down on his vitriol, claiming he was ready for “war” if necessary. He then claimed that Fif ain’t nobody to actually fear and that the people around him are the men who have put in real work in the streets. “Owwwwww what I ran into 50 many times he only had monster around him that was risking his life and really holding the line!…Nobody trusting 50 he too vicious…ni**a spoke on my child like this ni**a mentally sick lol,” he wrote back.

It seems 50 Cent has been attacking Diddy forever. However, the Power creator has turned up the trolling since Sean Combs was smacked with a litany of sexual assault and abuse lawsuits beginning with Cassie in November 2023. Curtis Jackson mocked the man for the allegations against him and even declared that he was working on a documentary detailing Diddy’s dirty deeds.
