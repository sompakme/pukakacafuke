Felicity Huffman News - Us Weekly

Publish date: 2024-07-05

Name:Felicity Huffman

Hometown:Bedford, NY

Birthday:December 9, 1962

About

One of the bevy of forty-something naughty ladies on Desperate Housewives, Felicity Huffman was born in 1962 in Bedford, N.Y.

She graduated from New York City’s Tisch School of the Arts and transitioned into an actress who’s selected unusual roles — including her Golden Globe-winning role as a transsexual in 2005’s Transamerica.

For her well-received portrayal of Lynette Scavo on Housewives, Huffman has earned an Emmy Award and numerous Golden Globe nominations.

She is married to actor William H. Macy and the couple has two daughters together.
