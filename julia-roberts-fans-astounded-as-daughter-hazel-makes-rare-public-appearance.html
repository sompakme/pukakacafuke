Julia Roberts Fans Astounded As Daughter Hazel Makes Rare Public Appearance

Publish date: 2024-09-16

Julia Roberts' daughter Hazel, 16, has turned heads after making a rare public appearance at the Cannes Film Festival.

The teen made her red carpet debut as she stepped out with cinematographer dad Daniel Moder for the premiere of Flag Day on Saturday.

Hazel looked beautiful as she wore a loose-fitting midi dress that had a yellow lace floral pattern.

Hazel completed the look by wearing a pair of black shoes and she also accessorized with a gold necklace. Her blonde locks were pulled back into a casual ponytail.

Daniel, meanwhile, looked handsome in a black suit and tie that he paired with a crisp white shirt for the occasion.

He served as Director of Photography on Sean Penn's father drama, which premiered at the Cannes Film Festival and will have a limited run in US theatres on August 20.

Pretty Woman star Julia shares twin daughter Hazel and son Phinnaeus, as well as son Henry, 14, with husband Daniel, and they are notoriously private about their family life.

The actress originally met the 52-year-old Emmy-nominated cinematographer on the 2000 set of the film The Mexican co-starring Brad Pitt.

She and Daniel tied the knot in 2002 and recently celebrated their 19th wedding anniversary on the Fourth of July this year with a rare beach selfie.
