Jeffrey Nordling Career

Jeffrey is an American actor who his major roles was playing Ted Orion, an NHL pro turned hockey coach in D3: The Mighty Ducks. Jeffrey starred alongside Kim Raver in the 1997 TV movie Soul Mates, shown by market researchers at Television Preview as a “new” pilot. He portrayed original Apple investor Mike Markkula in the TNT movie Pirates of Silicon Valley in 1999.

From 1999 to 2002 he played Sela Ward’s ex-husband, Jake Manning, on the TV drama Once and Again . He then went on to star as Brent Barrow alongside Courteney Cox in the series Dirt on the FX cable network in 2007–08.

In the 2009–10 season of Desperate Housewives, Jeffrey played Nick Bolen, a regular and the on-screen husband of Drea de Matteo, but the roles of the Bolens were discontinued after the one season.

Jeffrey starred as 9/11 hero Tom Burnett in Flight 93. He started playing a recurring role on the medical/crime drama Body of Proof in 2011 where he played Todd Fleming, the ex-husband of Dana Delany’s character, which he played until the show’s cancellation.
