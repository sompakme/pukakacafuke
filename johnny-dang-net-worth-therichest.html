Johnny Dang is a Vietnamese-American jeweler and entrepreneur who has an estimated net worth of $2 million.

Johnny Dang started his career when he worked as a watch repairman in a flea market located in Houston. He grew his stand into a two jewelry repair stores in Sharpstown Mall. He created custom bling-blings to a wide range of Hollywood celebrities and grills to a lot of rappers.

His fabulous creations were featured in several magazines and TV shows such as XXL, Ozone, MTV's Super Sweet Sixteen and BET's Spring Bling. Dang and his business partner, Paul Wall appeared on their own reality show, The Johnny Dang and Paul Wall Show. He was known as TV Johnny and Jeweler to the Stars.

Johnny Dang was born on March 11, 1981 in Houston, Texas.
