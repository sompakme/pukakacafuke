10 TV Couples You Didn't Know Are Together In Real Life

Publish date: 2024-07-14

The chemistry between two stars on tv can be so good, fans believe they would be a match made in heaven in real life. Surprisingly, numerous television couples really are actually together and some have even been married for years!

RELATED: 10 Celebrity Couples Who Has Their Marriages Annulled (&Why)

Fans are shocked to learn that the tv couples on this list are continuing their romance when the cameras are off. Let's take Parks and Recreation stars Nick Offerman and Megan Mullally, who play Ron and his ex-wife Tammy. Not only have they made us laugh with their wild relationship on the show, they have actually been together in real life for 17 years.

Stranger Things stars Natalia Dyer and Charlie Heaton share a remarkable on-screen chemistry on the Netflix series and in real life, too. The duo have been dating since 2017, making their romance official on red carpet events and social media.

No wonder these tv couples share an undeniable romance on-screen because off-screen, they are actually together in real life!

10 William Daniels And Bonnie Bartlett

William Daniels and Bonnie Bartlett have starred together in Boy Meets World, starring as Mr. Feeny and Dean Bolander, and on St. Elsewhere as Ellen and Dr. Mark Craig. The two loved working with one another, especially getting to act in two different shows together. In real life, this duo has been married since 1951 and has two sons!

As one of the longest-married couples in Hollywood, Daniels and Bartlett have a secret to keeping their marriage strong. "We help each other, and we respect each other, and that's the ingredients I think that makes a successful relationship," Daniels touchingly shared.

9 Nick Offerman And Megan Mullally

Nick Offerman and Megan Mullally are known for their wild and hilarious romance on Parks and Recreation, and it's definitely the same in real life. These actors are actually married and have been for 11 years.

The two met in 2000 in Los Angeles during a rehearsal for a play and hit it off instantly by later getting married in 2003. Ever since then, the duo have been in shows together, commercials and have even written a book titled The Greatest Love Story Ever Told, which gives fans an inside look at their romance. According to Parade, this A-list couple refuses to be apart for more than two weeks at a time.

8 Natalia Dyer And Charlie Heaton

Stars Natalia Dyer and Charlie Heaton play the boyfriend-girlfriend duo Nancy Wheeler and Jonathan Byers on the hit Netflix show Stranger Things and their romance has only blossomed in real life after meeting on set.

According to Elle, the actors seemed to have been hiding their romance, but in 2017, they were spotted holding hands in New York City and have since been seen traveling to Paris, sharing kisses in public, and made their relationship red carpet official at the 2017 Fashion Awards in London. When talking to V Man, Heaton opened up about his relationship with Dyer and how wonderful it is to be with someone who works in the same industry. "Because we work in the same industry and have had similar trajectories, we've gone through it together. Sharing that does bring you closer," he shared.

7 Kit Harington And Rose Leslie

Kit Harington and Rose Leslie met on the set of Games of Thrones playing love interests Jon Snow and Ygritte. They played a couple on the HBO drama for three seasons, but in real life, this couple is already married and are expecting their first child!

According to Insider, the duo met for the first time when they were filming the second season of the show when Snow meets Ygritte as he travels beyond the Wall. While living with her and the people called the Free Folk, he ends up falling in love with Ygritte. In 2018, the pair got hitched in Scotland, and Leslie showed off her growing baby bump in a cover story for UK's Make magazine.

6 Steve And Nancy Carell

The Office star Steve Carell who plays Dunder Mifflin Regional Manager Michael Scott had a few love interests on the show that included his real-life wife Nancy Carell. Nancy appeared on a few episodes of the show, starring as Carol Stills, a real estate agent who quickly turned down a trip to Jamaica with Scott and a proposal.

According to Country Living, the pair met when Nancy took one of Carell's improv classes at Second City in Chicago, and when she wasn't taking his classes, she bartended at a bar across the street that Steve would stop by to talk to her. The pair later got married in 1995 and have two children together.

5 Anna Paquin And Stephen Moyer

Fans will recognize Anna Paquin and Stephen Moyer as Sookie Stackhouse and Bill Compton from True Blood. According to BuzzFeed, the two met while screen testing for the show in 2007 and apparently hit it off instantly off camera because they later tied the knot in 2010.

After celebrating 10 years of marriage, Panquin told Us Weekly that they continue to be each other's "best friends." "We want nothing but good things for each other," she continued, adding, "His successes are my successes and vice versa and we're just really lucky." The married Hollywood couple are also parents to 7-year-old twins Poppy and Charlie.

4 Jared And Genevieve Padalecki

Supernatural star Jared Padalecki can thank his show for introducing him to his wife Genevieve Cortese. The pair met on the set of the haunting series, and have now been together for 11 years, and married for nine.

When talking about making time for their marriage since they are both actors and traveling for work, Genevieve shared, "As boring as it sounds, we've found that the best way to reconnect is to schedule a date night (or day) as often as possible. Our goal is weekly, but that happens pretty much never. I'm happy if we can reconnect and do something fun together, just us, twice a month."

3 Charlie Day And Mary Elizabeth Ellis

Mary Elizabeth Ellis and Charlie Day both star in It's Always Sunny In Philadelphia, which is going on their record-breaking 15th season and is now the longest-running comedy on television. The pair have been working on the FXX show since it aired in 2005. In an interview with Us Weekly, Ellis explained that the key to their real-life marriage is communication.

Ellis stated, "I love getting to spend time with him and hang out with him and have lunch with him, share the, you know, crappy, catering lunch together." The couple tied the knot in 2006 and have a son named Russell. They are also one Hollywood couple that love sharing their affection for one another on their social media.

2 Matthew Rhys And Keri Russell

Keri Russell and Matthew Rhys met on the set of The Americans and starred opposite each other since 2013. Not only did the pair have their own love story on the show, the duo found love offset, too. In 2013, Russell separated from her husband, who she shares two children. After the separation, rumors swirled that the actresses and Rhys were having a relationship off-screen, and it wasn't until a year later that the rumors were true.

On and off the show, their love for one another is pure and authentic and today, Russell and Rhys are happily married and have a child together, a son named Sam.

1 Kelly Ripa And Mark Consuelos

Kelly Ripa and Mark Consuelos have an adorable love story and after getting married in 1996, are stronger than ever. The two met on the set of All My Children when Consuleos was cast as Ripa's love interest, which created an instant chemistry between the two stars.

After a year or dating, the pair tied the knot by eloping in Las Vegas and later welcoming three children together. Ripa and Consuelos constantly show each other affection on Instagram and there is no denying that these two have really found true love in Hollywood.
