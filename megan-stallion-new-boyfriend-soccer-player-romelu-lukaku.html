Megan Stallion New Boyfriend: Soccer Player Romelu Lukaku

Publish date: 2024-07-27

In February 2023, the Megan stallion stopped following her ex-boyfriend Pardi Fontaine on Instagram, leading to speculation that they had broken up.

And now the Grammy winner has started to meet up with Romelu Lukaku, a Belgian football player.

The “WAP” rapper was photographed with Belgian soccer player Romelu Lukaku during Lautaro Martinez’s wedding over the weekend in Lake Como, Italy.

The pictures show Megan, 28, and Lukaku, 30, both under the management of Roc Nation, standing close to one another during the party and placing their hands on each other’s arms. 

Fans on social media speculated about the appearance, wondering if it meant she and her boyfriend, Pardison “Pardi” Fontaine, had broken up.

When working on the Grammy-winning song “Savage” remix (with Beyoncé), which brought home two of the artist’s three awards, Megan met Fontaine. 

The rapper declared her love for Fontaine during an Instagram Live session in February 2021.

According to the Spanish publication Marca, Lukaku recently ended his five-year relationship with Belgian model Sarah Mens, with whom he had a 4-year-old kid named Romeo in 2018.
