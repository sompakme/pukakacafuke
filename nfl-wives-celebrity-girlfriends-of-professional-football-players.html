NFL Wives, Celebrity Girlfriends of Professional Football Players

Publish date: 2024-07-12

Kerry Washington, Gisele Bündchen and Ciara are just a few of the women who’ve been romanced by A-list NFL athletes over the years.

Bündchen is famous in her own right as a supermodel, but her love for now ex-husband Tom Brady was evident from the start of their relationship in the early 2000s.

She remained by the Buccaneers quarterback’s side when he briefly retired from the NFL in February 2022. However, when Brady unretired the next month, the twosome hit a rough patch, with a source exclusively telling Us Weekly that September that the pair were “not seeing eye to eye regarding his retirement.” They announced and finalized their divorce in October 2022.

Ciara, for her part, has also become a fixture in the NFL after marrying Russell Wilson in 2016, just a year after they started dating.
