The success of “Old Town Road” was unparalleled. It topped charts in multiple countries, including the coveted number one spot on the Billboard Hot 100 for a record-breaking 19 weeks. The immense popularity of “Old Town Road” earned Lil Nas X numerous accolades and achievements. The song achieved diamond certification, signifying sales of over 10 million copies, and became the fastest song in music history to achieve diamond status. It also received numerous nominations and awards, catapulting Lil Nas X into the mainstream music scene.

This groundbreaking single not only launched Lil Nas X’s career but also opened doors for him to explore new opportunities.
