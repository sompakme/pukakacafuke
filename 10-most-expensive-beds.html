Beds are essential in providing a resting place. After working all day, the best thing to do at night is get some good sleep. This rejuvenates the body for the next day. Although almost any type of bed can serve the purpose, there are some elegant beds which have been optimally designed. There are usually beds that can be spotted in celebrity homes. These masterpieces combine technology, fashion, art, design and luxury. A lot of time, effort, creativity and resources are usually allocated for the creation process to ensure perfection. This is the list of the 10 most expensive beds that the rich and famous retire to:

10. Sphere Bed, $50,000 

Designed by Karim Rashid, the Sphere Bed is an ultimate choice for almost all bedrooms. It has a TV set, champagne holder, mirrors, and an LED lights. The bed is available in a wide range of colors. It can also be customized according to personal preferences.

9. Starry Night Sleep Technology Bed, $50,000 

Those with snoring problems will without doubt fall in love with this elegant bed. It has been technologically designed to reduce snoring. By elevating the upper body by a few degrees, it cures snoring. It also has an iPod slot, internet connection and temperature settings.

8. Monarch Vi-spring Bed, $50,000

This bed has been optimized to ensure luxury. It has more than three thousand springs that gives you an unmatched cushioned feeling. In addition to this, the mattress is made from high quality and hand crafted fabrics. David Beckham is one of the many celebrities who own the Monarch Vi-spring Bed. 

7. Cosmovoide Bed, $60,000

The Cosmovoide is quite a unique bed. It consists of intelligent features that take the joy of sleeping in it to a higher level. It has a phone, DVD Player and a full home theatre system that includes a TV set. 

6. Majesty Vi-spring Bed, $84,425

The famous Jab Anstoez hand crafted this bed. It consists of more than six thousand springs that provide sustainable suspension. It’s made from cotton, cashmere and silk. There are also several layers of silver and gold added into the design.

5. Quantum Sleeper Bed, $ 160,000

Just from the name, you would expect James Bond to own this bed. However, this is not an over-statement. The Quantum Sleeper has amazing features like lock down, ventilation system, settings for intruders, motion sensors, toilet, and even oxygen sensors. 

4. Parnian Furniture Bed, $210,000

The Parnian Furniture Bed was hand carved by Abdolhay Parnian. It took close to a year to finish it. The bed has numerous extra features like Swivel TV, iPad Holders and secret compartments. It’s made from wood, stainless steel and gold.

3. Jado Steel Style Gold Bed, $676,550

Made from gold and Swarovski crystals, the Jado Steel Style Gold Bed offers unmatched comfort ability. It has features like: Internet connection, PlayStation, DVD Player and a sound surround system. The design brilliantly combines technology and luxury.

2. Magnetic Floating Bed, $1.6 million

This magical bed floats in the air. The designer, from Netherlands, has incorporated magnets, weighing more than 1500 lbs to keep the bed floating. There are also strong wires that tie the bed to the wall to prevent it from drifting away. 

1. Baldacchino Supreme Bed, $6.3 million

Currently, this is the most expensive bed available in the market. It was hand carved by Stuart Hughes. It’s made from three types of wood namely: Ash, Cherry and Classy Canopy. The interior of the bed is accessorized by 24 carat gold that weighs more than 200 lbs. There are only two Baldacchino Supreme’s ever created.
