Fabio's Love Life Revealed; Here's What He Has To Say About Dating

Publish date: 2024-08-22

Described by many as being the king of romance, Fabio Lanzoni, most commonly just referred to by his first name, was viewed by many as being a sex symbol in the 90s. His unique look, and his flourishing modeling career helped to catapult him to fame, but in spite of his incredibly striking good looks and his fame and fortune, Fabio struggled to find the perfect lady, and is back in the dating scene once again.

Fabio, who is now 62-years-old, has played all the dating games for a while, and he now has a full appreciation and understanding for the type of girlfriend he is seeking to spend his time with. He's learned a lot about love and dating, and he's now viewing things differently than he once did. Yahoo reports that the heart-throb is seeking love and is determined to find it.

10 He's Still Searching For Mrs. Right

Fabio has definitely had his fair share of ladies, but he has yet to find 'the one.' He has indicated that he does believe that Mrs. Right is out there somewhere, and he's looking forward to finding her, and keeping her! Fabio has some ideas in mind as to what sort of lady would be the one to steal his heart for good - all he has to do now is just find her!

9 He Longs For His Lost Love

In spite of being idolized by many, it seems that there was one lady in his life that Fabio just couldn't hold on to. The Daily Mail reports on the fact that he was once in love with a woman but he managed to fumble and now he yearns over her. She was definitely 'the one that got away,' and this continues to haunt him today. Fabio hasn't revealed much about this woman, other than to say she was a model.

8 His Sports Cars Highlight His Current Dates

Everyone seems to have a special trick up their sleeves when it comes to their date nights, and for Fabio, that 'something' happens to come in the form of luxury cars. He currently owns well over 31 expensive sports cars, luxury cars, and supercars, and he definitely puts them to good use when he's trying to impress the ladies on a date. Fabio's dating game definitely involves some expensive car features!

7 Finding Girlfriends Is Easy For Him

While he has yet to find 'the one', Fabio has definitely admitted to finding a date pretty easily. In fact, when asked what his dating life was really like, he commented by telling Yahoo News; "There is quantity, but I want quality." Now his fans are well aware of the fact that finding a date is not his issue, but finding a woman to keep for the long run, seems to be where Fabio's dating life stops short.

6 He Is Seeking A Marital Relationship

Perhaps one of the most incredible things fans have recently learned about Fabio's love life is the fact that he really wants to settle down and get married. He is seeking a mate to marry and he wants nothing more than to settle down with the love of his life. He has never been married, but that's certainly not for the lack of wanting to.

5 He Would Like To Have Kids

The 62-year-old star stunned fans when he revealed that in spite of getting on in age, he truly and sincerely wants to be a father. Fabio is obsessed with creating the perfect nuclear family by settling down with someone special and starting a family. When asked about his age being an issue, he disagreed entirely, and continued to be steadfast in his wish to become a dad.

4 His Girlfriends Must Love The Outdoors

One of the benefits of seeking love at an older age is the fact that Fabio now has a very firm grasp on what he is looking for in a life partner. One of the many personality traits that he considers to be quite important is the element of the great outdoors. Fabio loves to be outside, immersing himself in nature, so he'll need a lady by his side that can brave the elements and not be afraid of the bugs!

3 Social Media Obsessed Girlfriends Are A Firm 'No'

Fabio has also drawn a clear line as to what he is not willing to accept or tolerate from a new relationship. He is determined to find someone that shares his belief that life is meant to be lived, not documented by a series of selfies and social media posts. He is not a fan, and he doesn't engage on social media at all, so he wouldn't pair well with a lady that is excitable about selfies and sharing her life with others online.

2 He's A Much Better Boyfriend Now Than Ever Before

The lady love that Fabio unfortunately wasn't able to hold on to is someone he admits to not treating very well. He deeply regrets not being more kind, loving, understanding and accommodating with this woman and knows that he lost her because he treated her badly. He learned from that experience and the lucky lady that dates him next is going to reap the benefits of him being a much better boyfriend now than he ever was before.

1 He Believes In True Love

The traditional concept of finding that perfect person and falling truly and madly in love is a dream that Fabio still holds very near and dear to his heart. He really believes in true love and is a true romantic. He is passionate about finding the person that is going to be there for him, and with him, forever.
