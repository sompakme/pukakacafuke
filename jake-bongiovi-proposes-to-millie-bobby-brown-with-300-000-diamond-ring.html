Jake Bongiovi Proposes To Millie Bobby Brown With $300,000 Diamond Ring

Publish date: 2024-09-04

At only 19-years-old, Millie Bobby Brown is sporting a huge engagement ring on her finger, courtesy of her new fiancé Jake Bongiovi, 20, the son of legendary rock star John Bon Jovi.

The Stranger Things actress announced the news with a post to Instagram on Tuesday morning, flaunting her new ring while posing on the beach in Jake’s arms. "I've loved you three summers now, honey, I want 'em all,” she captioned the post, which was a reference to Taylor Swift’s song “Lover.”

The couple have been together for around two years. They were first publicly linked when they shared a selfie together on Instagram in June 2021, and later seemingly confirmed their relationship status by posting a kissing selfie in November.

There are few details available about the ring Jake used to propose to Millie, but it’s estimated to be worth a small fortune. Jessica Flinn-Allen, CEO of wedding and engagement ring specialists Jessica Flinn, estimates the ring is easily worth over $300,000 USD.

“From what we can see in Millie’s Instagram photo, we’re guessing that the ring is valued in the region of £200,000 to £250,000,” the ring specialist explained.

“The ring is a cushion-cut solitaire 4ct diamond ring, and potentially a hugging halo on a polished band,” she added.

In November 2022, Millie was asked during an interview how she met Jake, and she replied that it was online. Moreover, the pair were only friends first after meeting. “We met on Instagram, and we were friends for a bit, and then, what can I say?” she shared.

Millie isn’t the only star to recently announce their engagement by showing off a huge wedding ring. Earlier this month, Lana Del Rey was wearing a diamond on her wedding finger.

Like Millie’s ring, Jessica Flinn estimated that Lana’s diamond could be worth around $300,000.

“The engagement ring is a traditional and timeless setting style, a three-stone ring in white metal, most likely platinum,” she said of the sparkler. “The ring features a large, round diamond in the centre, flanked by two smaller round-cut diamonds on either side, creating a symmetrical and elegant design.”

“The side stones on Lana Del Rey's engagement ring not only add to the symmetry and overall beauty of the ring, but they also serve an important purpose in enhancing the sparkle and brilliance of the centre stone,” she added.

If one thing is for sure, both of these engagement rings are stunning.
