Career

Eddie Bravo dedicated his career to the art of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, making significant contributions to the sport. He developed innovative techniques like the Twister and the Rubber Guard, which revolutionized jiu-jitsu and influenced practitioners worldwide.

To further promote jiu-jitsu and create a platform for showcasing talent, Bravo established the Eddie Bravo Invitational (EBI) grappling competition. The EBI has become a highly anticipated event, attracting top-level athletes and showcasing their skills in a competitive format.

Contributions to Jiu-Jitsu Innovation

Eddie Bravo has made significant contributions to the world of Jiu-Jitsu through his innovative techniques and the creation of the 10th Planet Jiu-Jitsu system. His dedication to pushing the boundaries of the sport has revolutionized Jiu-Jitsu and had a lasting impact on practitioners worldwide.

One of Bravo’s most notable contributions is the development of the rubber guard technique. This technique involves using the legs to control the opponent’s posture and create offensive opportunities from the bottom position.
