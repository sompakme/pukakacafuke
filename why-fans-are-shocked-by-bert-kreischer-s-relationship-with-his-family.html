Why Fans Are Shocked By Bert Kreischers Relationship With His Family

Publish date: 2024-08-18

While many families do legitimately love spending time together and often look forward to holidays and gatherings, many people would admit that even though they love their relatives, they sometimes have conflict. That is why family drama is such good fodder for TV shows and movies, like the actual family drama that led to The Family Stone.

Family drama, or just the stuff of daily family life, is also great material for comedians, and many talk about their spouses and/or children in comedy specials or during stand-up. It is also fun to learn more about a comedian's family, as fans love hearing about Jerry Seinfeld's wife and children.

Fans are shocked by Bert Kreischer’s relationship with his family, so let's take a look.

Updated on April 27, 2023: In an industry where divorce runs rampant, it is always encouraging to see thriving love stories. Bert and LeeAnn Kreischer have been married for 19 years, and will be celebrating their 20th wedding anniversary this December. Not only do they prioritize their marriage, but they are also intentional about putting family first with their two daughters.

Despite his political views, Kreischer seems to have fans from both sides of the aisle, as he just released his third Netflix special Bert Kreischer: Razzle Dazzle earlier this year. He is also currently on a stand-up tour, having performed in Melbourne, Australia just last week.

Are The Kreischers A Close Family?

Podcasts have become really popular in recent years, and there is nothing better than when a celebrity starts a fun podcast. Bert Kreischer’s wife, LeeAnn, has a really great podcast, and it has shed light on their family dynamic.

In an episode of the Wife Of The Party Podcast, Bert and LeeAnn talked about their family life, and it was a great opportunity for fans to learn more about them.

The best part of the episode? The fact that Bert and LeeAnn's kids, Ila and Georgia, were on it as well, and it is clear that they are all super close. They have a great bond and truly love spending time together, and their connection is clear from this podcast episode.

LeeAnn shared that they were excited for Bert's movie premiere, and their daughter joked that they could use leopard print sheets and pretend that it was a red carpet. LeeAnn said that the family could wear nice pajamas or sweatpants and joked, "I don't think you guys remember how to wear real clothes."

LeeAnn asked if her kids had a good Christmas and asked them what their favorite gifts were. Ila said getting an iPhone was great, and Georgia said she loved getting a sweater from her mom. She joked that it sounded "dorky," but she thought it was great, and LeeAnn said she was happy to hear that her daughter loved her gift so much.

The podcast episode is so heartwarming, and it is really nice to hear the family chatting and joking around together.

Bert then said that LeeAnn bought Ila a new pair of jeans, and she did no't like them, and Bert joked that she thought, "Screw you woman, I will not take a present from you." Ila said "That's not what happened" and laughed. Georgia said that whenever both sisters are asked to do something and help out, Ila thinks that Georgia will do it, and LeeAnn joked, "Ila has learned the path of least resistance."

Later on in the podcast, LeeAnn said that Buffalo Wild Wings was delivered to their house, and everyone was excited to enjoy some. LeeAnn said "energetically, you seem a little lighter" and asked her kids how they were feeling.

Georgia said something that a lot of people can likely relate to: "It's really weird because I've noticed that it's harder to jump into social things because you're so used to being with the same people..."

How He Prepared For The Cabin With Bert Kreischer

Bert's Netflix show The Cabin with Bert Kreischer saw him living in a cabin near Malibu, California, as he needed some time and space away from the busy, stressful grind. Bert and LeeAnn got married in 2003, according to Nicki Swift, and the publication says that LeeAnn records and produces her podcasts from the "man cave" that belongs to Bert, which is funny and heartwarming.

According to Paste Magazine, the show sees Bert hanging out with his friends in each episode, and sometimes he would talk with LeeAnn and their daughters via video chat, which sounds so charming.

Bert and his family are adorable together, and it is hilarious to listen to this whole podcast episode as they joke around and make fun of each other. Later on, near the end of the podcast, Bert started talking about accents, and the whole family pretended to have a French accent. They discussed how it is interesting that when people move somewhere they tend to lose their accent, and Bert countered with the idea that they are gaining a different accent.

This podcast episode is not only funny and a great way to spend a little over an hour, it is also a way for Bert's fans to connect with him and learn more about his wife and two daughters. If anyone was curious about Bert's relationship with his family, they have definitely learned that the Kreischer clan is super close and loving, and it is really endearing to see them together.
