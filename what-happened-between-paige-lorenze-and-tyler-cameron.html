What Happened Between Paige Lorenze And Tyler Cameron

Publish date: 2024-09-07

Bachelor nation star Tyler Cameron has had quite the colorful love life. Since making his reality TV debut on Hannah Brown’s season of The Bachelorette, the 29-year-old general contractor has dated a string of celebrities, including MTV star Juliette Porter, acclaimed supermodel Gigi Hadid, Bachelor nation alum Kristina Schulman, and model Camila Kendra.

After a year-long hiatus, Cameron finally dove back into the dating pool in July. Tyler and his new beau, model Paige Lorenze, were spotted flaunting PDA around New York City on multiple occasions, sparking relationship rumors. However, barely a month after going public with the relationship, Lorenze and Cameron decided to call it quits. Here’s everything we know about the Bachelor nation couple’s relationship and ultimate split.

8 When Did Tyler Cameron And Paige Lorenze Start Dating?

Tyler Cameron and Paige Lorenze started dating back in July after being introduced by mutual friends.

"[Tyler Cameron and Paige Lorenze] have been hanging out and hooking up,” a source close to the couple revealed to Entertainment Tonight at the time. “Things are casual, but they're having fun and there's an attraction between the two of them. They've hung out in groups with their friends and everyone gets along and has been having a good time."

7 When Did Paige Lorenze And Tyler Cameron Break Up?

Tyler Cameron and Paige Lorenze’s whirlwind romance ended as abruptly as it started. Barely a month after making the relationship Instagram official, the two decided to go their separate ways.

“We actually had to take a step back,” Cameron told Daily Pop on August 8. “It wasn’t the right time. It wasn’t good for us. So, we’re back to not dating anymore,”

6 Why Did Paige Lorenze And Tyler Cameron Break Up?

Back in July, Tyler Cameron and Paige Lorenze seemed completely enamored with each other. The two even took a romantic “date cruise” to Nantucket and appeared unable to resist posting each other on social media.

Despite this initial enthusiasm, the new relationship proved too engaging for Cameron. "I can't figure it out," he disclosed to Daily Pop in August. "I guess just too much on my plate."

5 Tyler Cameron Claimed That His Split From Paige Lorenze Was Amicable

Despite their abrupt split, Tyler Cameron and Paige Lorenze still hold each other in high regard. Back in August, Cameron disclosed to Daily Pop that he and Lorenze parted ways amicably after arriving at the mutual conclusion that it was “just not the right time” to have a relationship.

According to Cameron, he and Lorenze still “have tons of respect and love for each other” despite their surprise split.

4 Paige Lorenze Was Blindsided By Tyler Cameron’s Breakup Announcement

Tyler Cameron seemingly forgot to discuss his breakup announcement with Paige Lorenze. Following the announcement, the model took to social media to clarify that she “had no idea [Tyler] was talking to media” and found the announcement "weird."

Despite being caught off guard, Lorenze later admitted to PageSix that “there wasn’t a dramatic split or any bad blood” between herself and Cameron.

3 Paige Lorenze Accused Tyler Cameron Of Dating Her For Media Attention

Barely a month after their breakup, Paige Lorenze made a startling accusation against Tyler Cameron. During an interview with PageSix, the model insinuated that Cameron leveraged their relationship and ultimate split for publicity.

“He was looking at my [Instagram] Story views, and I was getting more views than him, and he was like ‘I need a scandal.'”

2 Paige Lorenze Felt Used By Tyler Cameron

Paige Lorenze also claims that Cameron’s desire for media attention significantly influenced his decision to go public with their relationship.

“This might sound narcissistic because he has a pretty insane following, but I felt like he was using me,” she disclosed to PageSix in September. “I think he saw an opportunity, not that he didn’t like me, but I think that’s why he wanted to go public so fast.”

1 What Next For Paige Lorenze After Split From Tyler Cameron?

Paige Lorenze is seemingly taking her abrupt split from Tyler Cameron in stride. The model has chosen to focus on herself and running her clothing brand, DairyBoy.

“I’ve always made such large life adjustments for my partners in the past,” she disclosed to PageSix, “but for right now, focusing on me is where I am at. It feels good to finally be comfortable with being selfish and putting myself and my future first.”
